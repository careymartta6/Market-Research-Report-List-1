<p><p>A water heater expansion tank is a small, water-filled tank that is installed within a plumbing system to accommodate the thermal expansion of water as it heats up. When water is heated, it expands, potentially creating excess pressure in the plumbing system. The expansion tank acts as a cushion, absorbing this increased pressure to protect pipes and appliances from damage. </p><p>The Water Heater Expansion Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
