<p><strong>The Photographic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Pass Filter</li><li>Short Pass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photographic filter market comprises various types, notably long pass and short pass filters. Long pass filters transmit wavelengths longer than a specified cutoff, enhancing colors and reducing UV interference, making them ideal for landscape photography. Conversely, short pass filters allow wavelengths shorter than a designated limit, effectively blocking longer wavelengths, which can create dramatic effects and enhance details in subjects like flowers or skin tones. Together, these filters serve distinct purposes in creative photography and image correction.</p></p>
