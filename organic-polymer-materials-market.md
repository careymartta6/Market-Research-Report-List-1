<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Polymer Material</li><li>Thermosetting Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic polymer materials market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting polymer materials. Thermoplastic polymers can be melted and reshaped multiple times, making them versatile for various applications like packaging and automotive parts. In contrast, thermosetting polymers undergo a chemical change when heated, resulting in a rigid structure that cannot be remolded, which is ideal for applications requiring durability, such as electrical insulators and adhesives. Each type serves distinct functions based on their properties and processing methods.</p></p>
