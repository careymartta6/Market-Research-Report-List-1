<p><p>The air-cooled chiller market is primarily categorized into two types: air-cooled scroll chillers and air-cooled screw chillers. Air-cooled scroll chillers utilize a compact design with fewer moving parts, providing reliable cooling efficiency for smaller applications. In contrast, air-cooled screw chillers employ a scroll compressor, allowing for higher capacity and greater energy efficiency, making them suitable for larger industrial uses. Both types are prevalent for their ease of installation and maintenance, catering to diverse cooling needs in various sectors.</p></p>
