<p><p>Duct heaters are essential in HVAC systems, categorized primarily into three types. Terminal air heaters are installed at the end of ductwork, providing localized heating for specific spaces. Main air heaters are positioned centrally to condition air for broader distribution throughout a building. Process heaters, on the other hand, are utilized in industrial settings to maintain optimal temperatures for specific processes, ensuring efficiency in manufacturing and production. Each type serves distinct heating needs within various environments.</p></p>
