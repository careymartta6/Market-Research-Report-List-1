<p><ul><li>Upright Microscope</li><li>Inverted Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorized microscope market includes two primary types: upright microscopes and inverted microscopes. Upright microscopes are designed for specimens that are flat and can be placed on a slide, making them ideal for biological and material science applications. In contrast, inverted microscopes have objectives positioned above the stage, allowing observation of larger samples or living cells in culture dishes from below. Both types utilize motorized features for enhanced precision and automation in imaging and analysis, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>
