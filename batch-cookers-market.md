<p><p>Batch cookers are categorized into manual and automatic types based on their operation. Manual batch cookers require hands-on intervention for cooking processes, allowing chefs to control time and temperature precisely, making them ideal for customized recipes. In contrast, automatic batch cookers streamline the cooking process with programmable settings, offering consistency and efficiency by reducing labor involvement. Both types serve different market needs, with manual cookers favored in artisan or small-scale kitchens, while automatic models are preferred in larger operations for productivity.</p></p>
