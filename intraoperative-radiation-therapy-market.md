<p><p>The intraoperative radiation therapy (IORT) market primarily includes Electron IORT and Intraoperative Brachytherapy. Electron IORT utilizes high-energy electron beams delivered directly to the tumor site during surgery, minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. In contrast, Intraoperative Brachytherapy involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, allowing for a concentrated dose of radiation while reducing systemic exposure. Both techniques enhance precision and efficacy in cancer treatment, improving patient outcomes and potentially reducing treatment times.</p></p>
