<p><strong>The Industrial Thermostatic Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-way Thermostatic Control Valves</li><li>2-way Thermostatic Control Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Thermostatic Control Valves market comprises two main types: 3-way and 2-way thermostatic control valves. 3-way thermostatic control valves manage the flow of fluids to maintain desired temperature levels by mixing or diverting flow between heating and cooling sources. In contrast, 2-way thermostatic control valves regulate fluid flow within a single system, allowing for precise temperature maintenance by opening or closing based on temperature changes. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency and process control in industrial applications.</p></p>
