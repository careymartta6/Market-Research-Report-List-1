<p><strong>The Standard Recovery Rectifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Standard Recovery Rectifier</li><li>SMD Standard Recovery Rectifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Standard Recovery Rectifier market is categorized into two main types: Axial Standard Recovery Rectifiers and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Standard Recovery Rectifiers. Axial rectifiers feature long leads for through-hole mounting, making them suitable for traditional circuit designs. In contrast, SMD rectifiers are compact with flat profiles, facilitating high-density circuit layouts and automated assembly processes. Both types are essential for converting alternating current to direct current in various electronic applications, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in power management solutions.</p></p>
