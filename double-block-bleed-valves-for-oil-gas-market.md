<p><p>Double Block & Bleed (DBB) valves are critical components in the oil and gas industry, designed to enhance safety and reliability by isolating sections of piping. This configuration is essential for preventing leaks and ensuring the integrity of systems in high-pressure environments, making them increasingly vital in upstream, midstream, and downstream operations.</p><p>The Double Block & Bleed Valves for Oil & Gas Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period.
