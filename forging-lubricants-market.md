<p><strong>The Forging Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soap Based Lubricants</li><li>Water Based Lubricants</li><li>Oil Based Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forging lubricants market comprises three primary types: soap-based lubricants, water-based lubricants, and oil-based lubricants. Soap-based lubricants offer excellent adhesion and stability, making them suitable for high-temperature forging processes. Water-based lubricants are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and reduce smoke during operations, though they may offer lower lubrication properties. Oil-based lubricants provide superior film strength and protection against wear, making them ideal for heavy-duty forging applications, but they can pose environmental concerns and require careful management.</p></p>
