<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors</li><li>EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors</li><li>PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 gas sensor module market comprises several types, including NDIR, EC, PAS, and others. NDIR sensors utilize infrared light absorption to detect CO2 concentration, known for their accuracy and stability. EC sensors employ electrochemical reactions, offering compact designs but are generally less precise. PAS sensors analyze sound waves to measure gas concentration, providing rapid and sensitive detection. Other sensor types may include infrared LED or optical methods. Each type has unique advantages catering to various applications.</p></p>
