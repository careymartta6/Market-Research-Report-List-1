<p><p>The steroid injection market is segmented into four primary types: intra-articular, intramuscular, intravenous, and epidural. Intra-articular injections deliver steroids directly into joints, providing localized relief for conditions like arthritis. Intramuscular injections administer steroids deep into the muscle, often used for systemic effects in various disorders. Intravenous injections introduce steroids directly into the bloodstream, allowing for rapid absorption and action. Epidural injections target the epidural space around the spinal cord, offering pain relief for back and nerve-related issues. Each type addresses specific medical needs effectively.</p></p>
