<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Physical Vapor Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vapor deposition market is primarily divided into two types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD involves the chemical reaction of gaseous precursors to form solid materials on substrates, commonly used for producing thin films with high uniformity and quality. In contrast, PVD utilizes physical processes, such as evaporation or sputtering, to deposit materials from a solid or liquid source onto surfaces. Both techniques are essential for applications in semiconductor manufacturing, coatings, and optoelectronics.</p></p>
