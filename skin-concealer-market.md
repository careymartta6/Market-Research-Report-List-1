<p><p>The skin concealer market is primarily divided into two types: concealer cream and concealer liquid. They are ideal for those seeking long-lasting, heavy-duty performance. On the other hand, liquid concealers are lighter and often more versatile, suitable for broader application and natural-looking coverage. Liquid options tend to be buildable and are favored for their ease of blending and brightening effects.</p></p>
