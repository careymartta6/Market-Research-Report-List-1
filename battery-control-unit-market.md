<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Control Unit (BCU) market comprises three primary types: distributed, centralized, and modular systems. Distributed BCU systems manage individual battery cells or packs, enhancing flexibility and reliability through localized control. Centralized BCUs oversee multiple battery units from a single point, streamlining management and reducing complexity but potentially increasing response time. Modular BCUs feature interchangeable components, allowing for easy upgrades and scalability, catering to varying energy storage needs while maintaining efficient battery performance and monitoring.</p></p>
