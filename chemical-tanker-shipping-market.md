Coastal chemical tankers (5,000-9,999 DWT) operate along coastlines, facilitating trade between nearby ports. Deep-sea chemical tankers (10,000-50,000 DWT) are designed for long-distance voyages, moving larger volumes of chemicals internationally, connecting producers to global markets efficiently. Each type caters to distinct logistical needs.</p></p>
