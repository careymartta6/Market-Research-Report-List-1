<p><p>Shared bicycle services are categorized into two primary market types: docked and dockless. Docked systems require bicycles to be returned to fixed stations, facilitating better management and order. Users unlock bicycles using a smartphone app or card at these stations. In contrast, dockless systems allow users to pick up and drop off bicycles anywhere within a designated area, providing greater flexibility. This model often relies on GPS technology for locating bikes and can enhance accessibility, attracting a wider range of users.</p></p>
