<p><p>The fire-tube package boiler market includes three primary types: D-type, A-type, and O-type package boilers. D-type boilers feature a drum positioned with a horizontal orientation, promoting efficient steam generation. A-type boilers have a design resembling the letter "A," suitable for high capacities and enhanced heat transfer. O-type boilers, characterized by their circular drum configuration, provide a compact design ideal for tighter spaces. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, optimizing performance and efficiency in steam generation applications.</p></p>
