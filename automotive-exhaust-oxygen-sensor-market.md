<p><p>The automotive exhaust oxygen sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: Titanium Oxide Type and Zirconia Type sensors. Titanium Oxide sensors operate based on the oxidation-reduction reactions of titanium, offering fast response times and high sensitivity, making them suitable for advanced engine management systems. Zirconia Type sensors, utilizing zirconium dioxide, are widely adopted due to their reliability and durability in high-temperature environments. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions in modern vehicles.</p></p>
