<p><ul><li>Monofilament Fiber</li><li>Multifilament Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibrillated polypropylene concrete fiber market primarily consists of two types: monofilament and multifilament fibers. Monofilament fibers are single, continuous strands that enhance the tensile strength and crack resistance of concrete by preventing shrinkage cracks. In contrast, multifilament fibers are made from multiple threads twisted together, providing improved dispersion and greater surface area for bonding within the concrete mix. Both types contribute to enhanced durability, toughness, and overall performance of concrete structures in various construction applications.</p></p>
