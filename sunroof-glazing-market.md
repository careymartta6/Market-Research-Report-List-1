<p><p>The sunroof glazing market encompasses three primary types: single glazing, double glazing, and triple low-E glazing. Single glazing consists of a single layer of glass, offering basic light transmission but limited insulation. Double glazing features two glass layers, providing enhanced thermal insulation and noise reduction. Triple low-E glazing incorporates three layers with low-emissivity coatings, maximizing thermal efficiency and minimizing heat transfer. Each type caters to varying preferences for energy efficiency, aesthetics, and performance in automotive and architectural applications.</p></p>
