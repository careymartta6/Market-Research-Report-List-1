<p><p>The Pellet Dosing Apparatus market is categorized into three main types: weigh, volumetric, and count dosing systems. Weigh dosing utilizes precise scales to measure the exact weight of pellets, ensuring accuracy in applications where weight is critical. Volumetric dosing measures and dispenses pellets based on volume, offering speed and efficiency, often used in bulk applications. Count dosing focuses on quantifying individual pellets, providing precise counts for applications needing specific quantities. Each type caters to different industry requirements in dosing accuracy and speed.</p></p>
