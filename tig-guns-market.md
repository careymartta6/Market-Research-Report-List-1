<p><ul><li>Water Cooled</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TIG guns market is categorized into two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled. Water-cooled TIG guns are designed for heavy-duty applications, using water to dissipate heat, which allows for longer welding durations without overheating. They are ideal for high-amperage processes. In contrast, air-cooled TIG guns rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more portable and easier to handle in less demanding tasks. Each type serves specific welding environments and user needs, influencing the market's dynamics.</p></p>
