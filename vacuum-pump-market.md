<p><ul><li>Wet vacuum pumps</li><li>Dry vacuum pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum pump market encompasses two main types: wet and dry vacuum pumps. Wet vacuum pumps use liquid to create a vacuum, often providing efficient gas handling and are ideal for applications like chemical processes. In contrast, dry vacuum pumps operate without fluids, minimizing contamination risks and maintenance needs, making them suitable for semiconductor and pharmaceutical industries. Each type addresses specific operational requirements, with wet pumps excelling in terms of efficiency and dry pumps prioritizing cleanliness and reliability.</p></p>
